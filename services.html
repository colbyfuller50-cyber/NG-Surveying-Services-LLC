Our Services

Boundary Surveys

We determine property corners and boundary lines using record research and field measurements, including preparation of a survey plat for recording.

Property Line Markings

Visible marking of property boundaries using stakes and flagging for fencing, landscaping, construction, and general awareness.

Closing Surveys

Mortgage surveys verifying property boundaries and improvements, supporting real estate closings, refinancing, and transfers.

Residential Construction Staking

Accurate field placement of site plans to ensure structures meet setbacks and construction proceeds with confidence.

Subdivision Surveys

Creation or adjustment of property lots in compliance with county and municipal standards, including subdivision plat preparation.
