## HConnect Formspree
1. Go to [Formspree.io](https://formspree.io)
2. Sign up with your email and create a new form
3. Copy your unique form endpoint (looks like `https://formspree.io/f/mabc1234`)
4. Open `request.html` and `contact.html`
5. Replace `https://formspree.io/f/your-form-id` with your unique link
6. Deploy your site — submissions will go directly to your email inbox!
