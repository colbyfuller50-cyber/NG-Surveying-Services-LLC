Our Services

Boundary Surveys

We determine and identify the locations of property corners and boundary lines using a combination of detailed research and review of record documents and field measurements. Includes drafting of a survey plat for recording

Property Line Markings

We visibly mark property corners and boundary lines on the ground using stakes, flagging, and markers. This service provides a visible representation of your property limits for projects such as fencing, landscaping, construction, or general awareness.

Closing Surveys

We perform closing (mortgage) surveys to verify the locations of property boundaries and all improvements such as buildings, fences, driveways, walls, etc. A detailed plat is prepared to support real estate closings, refinancing, and property transfers.

Subdivision Surveys

We provide Subdivision surveys to create new lots or adjust existing property boundaries in compliance with county and municipal standards. This service includes verifying existing boundary lines, establishing new lot configurations, and preparing a subdivision plat suitable for recording.

Topographic Surveys

We provide Topographic surveys to document the existing conditions of a property by measuring ground elevations and locating features such as buildings, driveways, utilities, drainage, and natural terrain. This service includes the preparation of a drawing file showing contours, spot elevations, and all mapped site features for use in engineering, design, and construction planning.
